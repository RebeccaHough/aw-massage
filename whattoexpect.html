Before your massage

  • Your first massage will include a consultation with Aase regarding any relevant health and lifestyle information so that you receive a tailor made treatment.
  • If you are unsure whether you can have a massage please check with your doctor before any treatment.
  • It is advisable to avoid a heavy meal within an hour prior to your massage.

During your massage

  • Most treatments are carried out directly on the skin using different oils, waxes and creams.
  • You will be draped in towels, except for the area being massaged.
  • You will be kept warm and comfortable at all times and soft music may be played to create the right atmosphere.
  • Aase will make sure that you are completely relaxed so that you receive the most effective massage.

After your massage

  • Depending on the treatment you've had and how your body responded to it, you might feel sleepy,lightheaded or energised, yet relaxed.
  • A 5-10 minutes rest on the couch afterwards will slowly bring you around.
  • After deep muscle treatment you might feel slightly sore for about a day but this should soon disappear making you feel better than before the treatment.
  • It's advisable to drink plenty of water as an increased lymph flow will flush out any released toxins.

Payment

  • Aase accepts payment by cash, cheque or bank transfer.

Travel Cost

  • There will be an additional charge of £4 for mobile service within a 10 mile radius.
